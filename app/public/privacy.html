INFORMATION WE COLLECT AND USE
When we refer to “Personal Data,” we mean any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person (“data subject”); an identifiable natural person is one who can be identified, directly, or indirectly, by reference to an identifier such as a name, an email, or location data like a home address.

HOAlly will collect such data when you sign up or join a community as necessary for use of the site's services.

HOAlly also collects and stores user provided content like proposals, announcements and comments as well as associated images or attached documents.

HOAlly operates in terms of closed communities of members, that may join only by invitation.

How HOAlly uses your personal information:
- We will not share your email address publicly on the site's pages or in any other way.
- Your name and your home address will be visible to other community members on communities that you join, whenever you make a post/announcement/comment, within the context of a community.
- Your name and home address will not be visible to other community members when you vote/like but there will be a stored in the HOAlly servers as this is necessary for the site's proper function (i.e. double voting).
- Your personal information and posts will not be visible to other HOAlly users or site visitors, who are not members of the communities that you have joined.
- You will be able to change or delete your personal information at any point via the site's functionality.
- As long as you have provided an email that you own, you will still be able to recover your account and perform the functions stated above even when you forgot your password.
- If you have signed up with an email that you don't own, you may not be able to perform the functions stated above (i.e. if you forgot your password). 
- We don't use any personal information or your posts or content for any other purpose than sharing it with other members of your joined communities.

How HOAlly uses your provided content:
- You may use HOAlly to post proposals or announcements and make comments and replies. Any of these forms or communications can also include attached images and documents.
- Your provided content may engage other community members that may vote/like or add their own comments and replies.
- Once you post such content you provide its full ownership to the HOAlly site and your community members.
- You may not be able to make modifications on such posted content.
- You will not be able to delete such content even if you remove yourself from a community or remove your HOAlly account.
- YOU MUST NEVER INCLUDE PERSONAL OR SENSITIVE INFORMATION in your posted content or attached images and documents.
- YOU MUST NEVER INCLUDE FULLY OR PARTIALLY COPYRIGHTED INFORMATION in your posted content or attached images and documents.

Other collected data
- HOAlly does not collect "Usage Data" regarding your use of the HOAlly site or other sites directly or through third-parties.
- HOAlly does not collect or use referral information like your interaction with social sites or other sites.
- HOAlly does not collect any data for marketing or advertisement purposes.

Use of Cookies
Strictly Necessary Cookies – These cookies are essential in order to enable you to login and move around the Website and use its features. These cookies will be placed on your device and used even if you do not consent to the use of cookies.
The cookies used by HOAlly are first party. First party cookies like the ones used when you log in to your account, are set by HOAlly and share the same domain.

HOAlly does not use any other cookies like Performance Cookies or Advertising Cookies.
HOAlly does not use third party cookies.
